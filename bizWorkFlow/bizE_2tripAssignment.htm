Trip Assignment

After Trip agreement is made by AARMSCM Solutions, a client can communicate his Trip Date, Time and number of vehicles required to the transporter using the Trip Assignment option in SCMBizconnect whenever the requirement arises.

A client prepares Trip Assignment using the below shown webpage on the SCMBizconnect. Trip date can be entered by selecting the date of requirement from the Calendar. After client does “Trip Assignment”, the information is sent to the transporter by email with the link “Click here to confirm”.
